BambooHR ist eine HR-Software, die Mitarbeiterdaten verwaltet. Sie unterstützt die Personalabteilung bei der Einstellung, Gehaltsabrechnung und Leistung.
BambooHR is all-in-one HR software made for small and medium businesses and the people who work in them—like you.
Our software makes it easy to collect, maintain, and analyze your people data, improve the way you hire talent, onboard new employees, manage compensation, and develop your company culture.
It's designed to set you free to focus on what matters most—your people.
